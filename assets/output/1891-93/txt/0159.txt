* Friday, November 6, 1891,  Pension Lejeune, Maximilianplatz 4, Vienna
We found nice rooms here taken for us all. 
Then we went to the Museum — !
x x x x x x x x x
“Faster, faster
O Circe goddess, 
Let the wild thronging train,
The bright procession
Of eddying forms
Sweep through my soul.” 
x x x x x x x x x
I had a nice long talk with Janet Morison in the afternoon.
Saturday, November 7, 1891, Vienna
At home with a dreadful cold. B. and Costa went to call on Wyckhoff. [sic] 
Sunday, November 8, 1891, Vienna
Cold worse. Could not use eyes. 
Janet to call.
