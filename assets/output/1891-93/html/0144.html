Letters came from “Michael Field” in the evening, and we annotated the Louvre Catalogue for Logan.

Tuesday, October 27, 1891, Hotel Croce d’Oro, Padua

Went to Duomo and Bishop’s Palace and discovered Montagnana!

Then to Santa Maria in Vanzo, then Sanmichele.

In the afternoon we finished our notes on the Gallery and went to the Scuola del Santo.
