Sunday, September 6, 1891, Hotel Roth, Munich

Went to the Pinacothek and studied and noted the Venetian and Veronese paintings.

Visited the various Brauereis in the evening, but they were so smoky that even the music did not tempt us to stay.

As it was a Volksfest, a cold rain was falling all day. God is evidently as undemocratic here as in England!
