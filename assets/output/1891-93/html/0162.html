Gallery. Called on Herr Hofrath von Enghert, Director.

Saturday, November 14, 1891, Vienna

Got in by myself and spent hours alone in the Gallery, and enjoyed myself beyond words. “Bleib – du bist so schön!!!”

Tea with Janet.

Sunday, November 15, 1891, Vienna

Finished notes at Academy.

Went to Richter concert.

[concert programme pasted down]

Went to drive with Giovanni Costa in the Prater.

Janet to tea.
