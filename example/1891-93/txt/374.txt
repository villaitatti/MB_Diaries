Bernhard began an article on Karl Frey’s book.We walked from the Bagno di Ripoli over the hills - a lovely walk - finding violets by the path.After tea, we wrote the tail to our Lotto, about his followers, etc., and after dinner I went on printing my Hawthorne, while Bernhard read Symonds on Italian Literature.
x  Wednesday March 8. 1893. Florence.
Finished and sent off the Hawthorne.Went to the Uffizi and studied particularly the engravings in the corridor.After lunch we finished the Lotto “Following”. Then Bernhard took a walk in the Cascine, while I looked at a little appartment.
