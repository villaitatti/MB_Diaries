 Saturday Nov. 28. 1891. Vienna
We went to the Albertina in the morning and finished our notes. Afterwards we wrote about the gallery, and went to Löwy’s.In the evening we wrote and read.
Sunday Nov. 29. 1891.
We went first to the Lichtenstein, but it was closed, so we went again to the Academy.At 12.30 we went to the Richter concert, whose programme is below. The clavier-concert was perfect enchanting, like a picture of Watteau - just the same spirit.In the afternoon Herr Wichkoff and Mrs. Morison called, and we wrote in the evening.
