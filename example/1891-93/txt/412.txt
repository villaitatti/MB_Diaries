*  Friday Aug. 18. 1893. Pfeiffer. Strassburg
Reached here about 1.30. Went to Museum and discovered 2 Carianis and a Beccafumi. Saw the Cathedral. It made an immense impression at first, and then we were disappointed. It is so barbarous compared to those we have been seeing. The Romanesque choir with the cupola, however, we enjoyed. We did not like the façade. Hot !
