Went over sculpture, etc., photographs in the morning.Walked in the Cascine in the afternoon.Read poetry of Charles d’Orleans, Perry, Ranke.Bernhard has just finished  Morley’s Diderot ,and is reading  Voltaire and  Tristram Shandy . Mrs. James, Loeser told us, had kept his dinner party waiting half an hour because the dressmaker had not sent home her “body” in time. The “body” turned out to be a red cashmere blouse.
