Saturday May 14. Train to Paris Milan
An awful day of heat and dust for me. The children enjoyed it.I reached Paris at 6.30 and went to Logan’s apartment, 14 rue de la Grande Chaumière.
I could not continue a separate journal. It was too dreary being away from Bernhard.
