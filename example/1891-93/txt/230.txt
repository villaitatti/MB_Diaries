*  Monday March 14. 1892 . The King’s Birthday. 
Being a popular holiday, with the Galleries closed, of course it poured. We arranged photos, all the morning.Loeser came in.In the afternoon we had a walk. Were too tired in the evening to do much, so I read and finished “ My Trivial Life and Misfortune ,”and B. went on with Creighton. He is reading Huysmans’  La Bas .
