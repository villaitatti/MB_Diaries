Wednesday, June 21 
[22]
. 1892.Paris
We studied the Sienese and Florentine pictures in the Gallerie [ sic ] de Sept Mètres with Burke in the morning, and after lunch took the Mikes to the New Salon.Burke came to tea, and after dinner Maude came and played to us on her violin and told us any quantity of funny stories. One was of a man who exaggerated fearfully. A friend remonstrated with him.
