Went to the Louvre and began our notes. I was not very well, and I felt very cross at meeting Bôcher unexpectedly. Bernhard lunched with him. I decided not to bear malice against him for his garrulity, so I wrote him a peace-making note.
Paris. Saturday Aug. 20.  92. 
Notes in the Louvre. Bôcher came to tea.
Sunday Aug. 21. 1892
Notes in the Louvre. I began to feel ill.Finished Creighton’s “ Simon de Montfort .”
Monday Aug. 22. ’92
I was much worse. But we went to Durand-Ruel’s with Bôcher and saw a new Besnard, Degas, etc.Went to the Exposition des Arts de la Femme, and amused ourselves over the old fashions.
