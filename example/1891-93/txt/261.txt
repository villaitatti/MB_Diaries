Bernhard very much enjoyed his visit to the Uffizi with Mlle Fleury. She told him that painters now-a-days have their architecture and perspective all drawn for them by people especially trained to it.
*  Tuesday April 19. ‘92 Viareggio
Bernhard came at 9.30, and I met him at the station. The history of our day is walks interspersed with writing our Venetians - or rather the other way about.It was gloriously sunny in the afternoon, and after a rest, we walked in the pine forest. The children were enchanted with the sand and the waves.
