The settle running all round the deep fire-place, and the country yokels who came in and sat there in the shadow of the chimney with the firelight on their faces - made an indescribably enjoyable ‘genre’ picture.

Tuesday Nov. 3. ’91. Italia - Udine

In the morning we saw several churches and finished the notes on the cathedral.

Then we came to Pordenone and saw his pictures in the Duomo and the town hall, and then came here.
