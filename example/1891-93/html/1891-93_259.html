* Friday April 15. 1892

Met Costa and Richter and Loeser in the Uffizi, and then went to see an interesting picture by Domenico Morone and assistants, which belongs to Dr. Richter.Mme Zucchelli and her daughter called, and we went to look at a house for next year - unsuitable.Bernhard called on Miss Britten and Miss Loope -Left a card on Mrs. Way.I had a little headache in the evening, but we went on with our work.

Saturday April 16. 1892.

A last look at the Uffizi. Afterwards much packing and a little work on the Venetians.We had tea at Bernhard’s room, and then left cards on Miss Britten (I) and Miss Madeleine Fleury (B.) who were both out.Worked in the evening.

Sunday April 17. 1892 Easter Sunday

We visited various churches in the morning and lunched together at the Toscana.Then we came back and worked till 5, and Bernhard went to find Miss Fleury.
