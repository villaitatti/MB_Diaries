Wednesday Sept. 10

[9]

1891. Colombe d’Or Verona

Embankment broken, so we had to change cars at 5.30, walking a long way. The scenery was very fine. At Alawe found our train had gone on and we had 4 hours, so we went to a hotel in the town and had breakfast and a sleep.Reached Verona at 4.20. Walked to San Zeno and saw the cloisters.Took a stroll after dinner.
