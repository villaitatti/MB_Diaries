It was worth coming to Munich if to see nothing but the Monets.After the Exhibition, we went back to Haufstängl’s and bought some photographs.Then came back, and while I read Faust, B. wrote a few pages about the Augustan marbles - which I criticised savagely before going to dinner.

Tuesday Sept. 9

[8]

. 1891. Munich – Verona

Milanese and Ferrarese Schools in the Gallery in the morning.B. lunched with Mr. Marshall.I read Charles Vand packed.Went to the International Exhibition in the afternoon. On the whole, we liked best the Besnard sunset over the water.
