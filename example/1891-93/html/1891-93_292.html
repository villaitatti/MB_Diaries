x Sunday Aug. 28. 1892. 14 rue de la Grande Chaumière. Paris

Finished the “Black Prince.”Read Mrs. Jameson, “Sacred and Legendary Art.” Read Morelli.Bernhard went to the Louvre.

Paris. Monday Aug. 29. 1892.

Read a story of Potapenko in La Revue Bleu. Also much more of Creighton and Mrs. Jameson.Enjoyed the pictures to the full.Began “La Débâcle.”Bernhard finished “Germinie Lacerteux.”

Tuesday Aug. 30. 1892. Paris

Read La Débâcle.Went to doctor and received a most discouraging report which made me very blue.Read Creighton.Hapgood came to tea.

Wednesday Aug. 31. 1892. Paris

Louvre in the morning.Met Hapgood and his friend Dodge, a Ruskinite.Lunched with Hapgood and then went to the Hotel de Ville and saw the Besnards, Le Rolleand Carnières.Saw Dr Remet.Read Creighton and La Débâcle.

Thursday Sept. 1. 1892. Paris

Feeling very ill - did not go out.Marion Lawrencecame and bored me nearly to death.Hapgood came in the afternoon and we went to the Pont d’Austerlitz and dined together.
