and the people - It was market day, and the “usual” Bassano was being enacted at every corner, cows and oxen, and copper pots and pans, and carts, and vegetables, and brightly dressed men and women bending over. It is really impossible to understand the Bassani without coming here, especially Jacopo.

Later we came to Castelfranco, and got just a glimpse of Giorgione’s Madonna before sunset. What a sunset – glowing long and long, like an American sunset, as we walked round the walls of the tiny town.
