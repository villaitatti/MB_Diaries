* Saturday April 30. ‘92 San Gimignano

We took notes in the Duomo in the morning, and in the afternoon walked out to a little Church about 3 miles out, called S. Andrea. There we found a fine Benozzo Gozzoli. The whole population of the village came in to the Church to watch us, silently but with determined curiosity. The little priest was too shy to invite us to have any refreshment. The walk was enchanting beyond words.We nearly finished our Titian in the afternoon and evening.
