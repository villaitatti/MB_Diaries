Tuesday. Jan. 17. 1893.

Uffizi. Opera del Duomo. Looked at photos.B. reading Voltaire and Tristram Shandy,I at Vol. II of Ranke and my German lesson.

Wednesday. Jan. 18. 1893.

Began our paper on Lotto, studied chronologically.Bernhard went to see Vernon Lee, and I went to see Mrs. James, who was so nice and sympathetic, as soon as she found out that I was not a Catholic.Sent “Solness” to Evening Post and Women’s Herald.

* Thursday. Jan. 19.

Still at work on our Lotto. It becomes very interesting.Bernhard walked alone, as I felt tired.Read Ranke, etc.

Friday. Jan. 20. 1893. Florence
