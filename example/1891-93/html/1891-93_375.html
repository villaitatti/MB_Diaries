Thursday March 9. 1893.

Went to the Uffizi and studied Florentine drawings.Worked on the Lotto. Finished Rosmersholm and compared it with Solness - Bernhard called on the Scotts and I went to San Miniato.After tea we grappled with the Lotto, and quarrelled, as usual when we write together! in the evening read “Vanitas,” which didn’t strike us as very bad. I also read the other two stories, one at night, and the other in morning.
