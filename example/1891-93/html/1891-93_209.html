I think we are clever enough to learn to write fairly well - I hope so - and B. at least has plenty to say. So have I, if I dared to utter all the rage that is in me. B. said “The beginning of activity is the unconsciousness of ignorance”- Still reading Golovleff, B. reading “Le Prêtre Marié”.

Tuesday Feb. 9. 1892. Florence.
