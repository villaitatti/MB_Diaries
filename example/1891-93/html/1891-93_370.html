> Wednesday March 1. 1893. Florence

Wrote my Hawthorne.Went to Santo Spirito and the Brancacci Chapel.Took the tram to Settignano and walked over the hills, a most delicious walk!Read in the evening.

x Thursday March 2. 1893.

Took Lady Mary and Mr. Murray to the Uffizi. They were nice. They said the others were still discussing the Zorn!Bernhard went to Santa Croce and the Bargello.Walked over the hills from the Bagno di Ripoli.Wrote.

Friday March 3. 1893.

Hawthorne still!Walked over behind Bellosguardo and enjoyed it.Read - wrote.
