Thursday Oct. 29. ’91. Croce d’Oro. Padova

Went to Santa Giustina in the morning and climbed up close to where we could see the Paolo, such a marvelous thing. We were both sick and dizzy from our poisoning, but we kept on and “did” the Scuola del Santo and the Church of S.
